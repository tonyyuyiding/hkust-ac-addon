This privacy policy applies only when you access the following URLs related to the HKUST AC system:

  • https://w5.ab.ust.hk/njggt/app
  • https://w5.ab.ust.hk/njggt/app/
  • https://w5.ab.ust.hk/njggt/app/home

We do not collect, use, or share any of your information when you access URLs not listed above.

What information do we collect

We only access tokens and identifiers generated by the HKUST AC System and its identification service. These tokens and identifiers are used solely for authentication that happens when you interact with the AC system through this extension.
No personal data, browsing history, or other information is collected, stored, or sold by this extension.

How do we use the information

The tokens and identifiers are used only to authenticate requests to the HKUST AC System, according to your input in the control panel provided by this extension.
We do not retain, store, or process these tokens and identifiers beyond the duration of the request.

What information do we share

The tokens and identifiers are read from your browser storage and shared only with the HKUST AC System when a request is made. We do not store or share this information after the request is completed. Under no circumstances will we share your information with any third party other than the HKUST AC System.

Your rights

You may remove this extension at any time to stop all data access.
